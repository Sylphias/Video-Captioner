## Decisions Made
- Diarization is opt-in via separate POST endpoint — transcription does not auto-trigger it
- NEVER use MPS device for pyannote — known accuracy regression on Apple Silicon; always CPU
- Diarization lifecycle returns to 'transcribed' on success — enriches transcript in place, no new terminal state needed
- pyannote-audio pinned to exactly 3.3.2 to avoid torch 2.8.0 incompatibility in 4.x
- HUGGINGFACE_TOKEN read from process.env at pipeline execution time, fails gracefully with descriptive error message
