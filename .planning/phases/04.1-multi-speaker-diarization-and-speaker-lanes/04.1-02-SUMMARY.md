## Decisions Made
- Speaker color index: `parseInt(sid.replace('SPEAKER_', ''), 10) % 8` — works for SPEAKER_00..N, wraps at 8 colors
- Phrase reassign assigns ALL words in phrase to new speaker for simpler UX
- useDiarize reloads store via `useSubtitleStore.getState().setJob()` inside the hook (not via a useEffect in the page) for clean separation
- Video preview uses existing `/api/jobs/:jobId/video` endpoint (backend already serves this)
